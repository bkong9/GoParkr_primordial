frequently asked questions.

Why Use Parkr?

As a driver, parking has never been easier! You can easily search for affordable & nearby parking downtown, during busy events, or whenever you might need it!

As a parking spot owner, a cheap & hassle-free way to generate income on the side!


How does listing my spot work?

Once you've registered, it's easy!

Simply navigate our accessible menu to list the availability of your spot, the amount you charge per hour, and any amenities your spot has!

To make it easier for potential parkers, we highly recommend uploading pictures of your spot.


Why Parkr over the competition?

Event Focused: we aim to help drivers connect with homeowners & small businesses to find parking during busy events! Our goal is to foster a mutually-beneficial relationship between the drivers & event organizers!

Current & Cashless: unlike other apps, we aim to have up-to-date parking prices that will change in real-time. In addition, we handle the payment process entirely & securely on our end, to lessen the hassle.

No Fees! While other apps charge 5-15%, we will be charging 0% for our launch period! Enjoy!
