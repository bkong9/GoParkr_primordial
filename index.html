frequently asked questions.

How do I get the app?

You can download the Parkr app for FREE in the Google Play Store.


How do I sign up?

Once the app has been downloaded, you can sign up with the following information: email address, mobile phone number, vehicle license plate number, and payment information.


How do I list a spot?

You can list a spot by clicking the “Earn Cash” button, and follow through the quick tutorial. Be sure to have the following items ready: email address, mobile phone number, bank information for payouts, picture of the parking spot, address of the parking spot.


Can a Reservation be cancelled?

Parkr will honor all refunds that are requested before the start time of your reservation. You can refund reservations on your own in the form of Parkr credits or a money back refund. Simply open the app, and go to “my trip” section to cancel a reservation. Parkr credit will be applied to the next reservation you book. A money back refund takes 2-3 business days to show up back to your card.


Do I pay for parking in advance?

Yes. Reserving with Parkr allows you to pre-pay for a guaranteed parking spot. Once you reserve a spot with us, you can drive to your destination knowing a parking space will be available for you. Our service lets you find parking locations often times with online discounts - and avoid any unexpected fees onsite.


How I I book a reservation?

On our mobile app, simply search the address of your destination. This will show you all of the parking locations we work with in the area. Each location will be listed in order of proximity, and you can find helpful information in the “Details” section. Simply set the "From" and "To" times at the top left of the page to suit your needs, and select “Book Now” when you find a great spot. Confirm all of the details at the checkout page, and then “Complete” to confirm the spot.


Can I add time to my session?

Yes, you can add time by going to your current session and tapping the “Add Time” button or change the end time. Parkr will also send out notifications prior to the expiration of your parking session.


How do I find a parking spot?

Simply type the address or the name of venue that you are going to into the search bar. If you are in a rush, simply tap the “$” or “KM” button and Parkr will offer you suggestion based on price or distance preference.


How do I find my parking location?

Each time you reserve a parking space, the further details of the space will be available at the “my trip” section of the app where you will be able to see the address, picture of the entrance, and instruction on how to access the parking space


Is my spot really reserved for me?

Yes, it is guaranteed. We partner with parking locations private owners to guarantee the availability of each parking spot on the appl. Even if a location is temporary marked full for drive-up customers, your Parkr reservation will still be valid, just drive up and get the attention of the attendant for assistance!


What if I don't have my vehicle description ready at checkout?

Vehicle description are the most important aspect of your profile. Especially at open lot locations where the space could be monitored by tow trucks. If you are unsure about your vehicle description, please enter TBD, and update it at anytime prior to your arrival at the parking space. You can update the vehicle description in the “My Profile” section or “My Trips”, “Update Vehicle Description”.


Will I have an assigned spot?

Unless specified on the detail page of the parking spot, you will not have a specific spot at the parking location, but you will be guaranteed a spot to park within the parking lot.


Will I have in and out access throughout my entire reservation?

In order to come and go as you please during a reservation, you will need to reserve a location where “in&out” is a privilege in the amenities section. You can also filter specific amenities at the bottom right of the main search home page.
